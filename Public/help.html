help Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a vehicle?

Simply navigate to the Fleet Discovery page, choose your preferred vehicle, and click 'Book'. You will be asked to select your pickup and return dates before confirming.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking for free any time before your scheduled pickup date. Go to 'My Bookings' and click the Cancel button next to your reservation.

How do VoltPoints work?

You earn VoltPoints for every successful rental you complete on our platform. These points are automatically tallied in your account and can be used for future upgrades.

Do I need to charge the EV before returning?

No! You can return the electric vehicle at any charge level. We handle the recharging at our main hubs at no extra cost to you.
